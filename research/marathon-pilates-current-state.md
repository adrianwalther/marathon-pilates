## Key Pain Points to Address (Observed)

1. **Private sessions booked via email** — not through Arketa. Major UX and operational gap.
2. **No online booking for privates** — clients must email, wait for reply, coordinate back-and-forth.
3. **Sauna + cold plunge sold separately** — no bundled checkout flow for class + recovery.
4. **Session length/capacity not published** — clients don't know how long a sauna session is or if it's private.
5. **Separate membership categories** — Pilates memberships and sauna memberships are siloed. No unified wellness membership.
