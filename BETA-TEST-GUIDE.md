# 🧪 Marathon Pilates — Beta Test Play-by-Play

Hi Ruby & Jazz! 👋 This is the new studio platform (the one that'll eventually replace Arketa). It's ready for a friendly first look.

**No rush, no pressure** — do it whenever you have ~15–20 minutes. The goal is simple: poke around like a real person would, and tell us anything that feels confusing, broken, or just *off*. There are no wrong answers — if something trips you up, that's exactly what we want to know.

Switch to your other browser (or incognito window) and **sign up fresh with a personal email** — go through it exactly like a brand-new member walking in off the street:

- [ ] **Sign up** + the **intake questionnaire** (the health/goals questions) — does it feel welcoming? Too long? Confusing?

- [ ] The **celebration message** that appears after a class is marked done *(Ruby — this is the "nice work today!" touch you asked for)*
